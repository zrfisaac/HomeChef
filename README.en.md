# <img src="Logo.png" alt="Logo" width="32" height="32"> HomeChef - Your Personalized Recipe Organizer

## UNDER DEVELOPMENT!

**HomeChef** is the ultimate recipe organizer for anyone who loves to cook! Save and categorize your favorite recipes, create meal plans, and keep track of ingredients all in one place. HomeChef makes it easy to organize recipes by cuisine, dietary needs, or prep time, so you can quickly find exactly what you’re craving. With a built-in pantry tracker, you’ll know exactly what ingredients you have on hand, helping you cut down on waste and simplify grocery shopping. Whether you’re a seasoned chef or just starting in the kitchen, HomeChef is your go-to app for delicious, organized cooking!

## Features
- **Recipe Storage**: Save and categorize your favorite recipes for easy access.
- **Meal Planning**: Create weekly meal plans and stay organized.
- **Ingredient Tracking**: Use the pantry tracker to monitor ingredients and reduce waste.
- **Advanced Search**: Filter recipes by cuisine, dietary needs, or prep time.
